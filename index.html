Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 2026

At "Meead", we put your privacy first. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on our own servers.

1. Data Storage

All data you enter (tasks, schedules, notes) is stored locally on your device only. If the sync feature is enabled, Apple's iCloud service is used to transfer data between your devices in a fully encrypted manner; we cannot access this data.

2. Required Permissions

• Notifications: For tasks, work shifts, and fingerprint reminders.
• Calendar & Reminders: To sync existing tasks or add new ones directly from the app.
• Photos: "Add Photos Only" permission to save copies of your schedules to your gallery upon your request.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not contain third-party analytics or advertising networks. It is completely free of tracking tools.
